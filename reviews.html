Excellent, organic program for learning a language.
★★★★★

Rosetta Stone uses the principals of psycho-linguistics to teach languages organically so that you are learning just as you would in the real world. Instead of mapping words out like a textbook, Rosetta Stone has you learn that word for what it is not as just a word. You see the picture and hear the word instead of having to go translate in your head back to your original language. It's a very sophisticated program, but it feels easy and fun.
